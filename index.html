What to Put in Your Soil

🌱 Nitrogen (Urea)

What it does: Makes leaves green and big

Types: Urea powder, manure

How much: 50-150 kg per acre

When: When crop is growing

🌾 Phosphorus (DAP)

What it does: Makes roots strong and flowers bloom

Types: DAP, SSP powder

How much: 20-60 kg per acre

When: Before planting

🪨 Potassium (Muriate)

What it does: Makes plant strong and stops diseases

Types: Muriate of Potash (MOP) powder

How much: 30-60 kg per acre

When: When crop is growing big

♻️ Natural Fertilizer

What it does: Makes soil healthy and long-lasting

Types: Cow dung, compost, neem cake

How much: 5-10 tons per acre each year

When: Before planting season

🐞 Stop Pests & Diseases

What it does: Keeps bad insects and diseases away

Types: Neem spray, chemicals for pests

How often: Every 2-4 weeks as needed

Important: Use safely - wear gloves

💧 Water Your Crop

What it does: Gives water for good growth

How often: Every 7-15 days

How much: 4-6 cm water each time

Best time: Early morning is best
